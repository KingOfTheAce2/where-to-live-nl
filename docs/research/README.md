## Executive Summary

Successfully identified and validated **free, open-access flood risk data** from the Dutch government for integration into the Where-to-Live-NL application.

### Key Findings

✅ **Data Source:** PDOK (Publieke Dienstverlening Op de Kaart)
✅ **API Type:** OGC WFS 2.0 (Web Feature Service)
✅ **License:** CC0 (Public Domain) - No restrictions
✅ **Cost:** FREE
✅ **Format:** GeoJSON (JSON-based geospatial data)
✅ **Quality:** Official government data from Rijkswaterstaat
✅ **Coverage:** All of Netherlands
✅ **Status:** Service verified and tested successfully
